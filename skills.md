🍫 Cocoa Krakye: skills.md
Version: 1.1 (The "Oseikrom" Update)
Mission: To protect yields and master the market by blending high-finance precision with the soul of West African cocoa culture. This isn't just a terminal; it's a digital Abinkyi for the modern cocoa stakeholder.

🌍 1. Hyper-Local Intelligence (The "Ground-Truth" Radar)
Skills focused on capturing the pulse of the Suhum valleys and the Western North highlands, translating bush-track data into boardroom strategy.

Village-Level Sentiment Mapping: Moving beyond macro stats to track "Farmgate Vibes"—using AI to aggregate reports from local LBCs on pod-counting, rainfall patterns, and farmer morale.

CSSV & Black Pod Spatial Analytics: Predictive heatmaps specifically tuned to the Ghanaian soil profile, identifying high-risk zones for Cocoa Swollen Shoot Virus before they hit the national news.

The "Galamsey" Impact Monitor: Geospatial tracking of illegal mining encroachment on cocoa concessions to predict long-term yield loss and soil degradation.

Ancestral Agronomy AI: A conversational agent trained on both modern BOC (Board of Control) standards and traditional Ghanaian farming wisdom to provide culturally relevant yield-boosting advice.

📈 2. The "Krakye" Financial Terminal (The "Vesper" Evolution)
Skills dedicated to the 'Krakye' (The Intellectual/Scholar)—bringing radical transparency to the $200B market with an African flair.

LID (Living Income Differential) Tracker: Real-time monitoring of the $400/tonne premium, ensuring investors and exporters see the true cost of "Ethical Ghana Gold."

Euronext & ICE "Adinkra" Dashboard: A high-end data visualization suite that maps London/NY price fluctuations against local COCOBOD announcements.

Exotic Bean Origin Provenance: Specialized tracking for high-value "Single Origin" lots, utilizing blockchain-lite verification to command premium prices for specialty Ghanaian beans.

Currency Fluctuations (GHS/USD/EUR): Predictive modeling on Cedi volatility and its immediate impact on local purchasing power and export margins.

📱 3. 'Read-on-the-Go' Experience (Refocused UI/UX)
Inspired by the uploaded design, we swap generic 'Lifestyle' for 'Life at the Origin.' High-end, dark-mode elegance meets the vibrant colors of a ripe cocoa pod.

Refocused Content Verticals (As seen in UI):

The Power Game: Strategic moves by the CMC (Cocoa Marketing Company) and regional price-fixing updates.

The "Krakye" Edit: Deep dives into sustainability (EUDR) and the future of chocolate tech.

Gold & Cocoa: Tracking the intersection of Ghana's two biggest exports.

Low-Bandwidth "Bush Mode": An ultra-efficient data-syncing skill that allows the app to refresh critical price data even on shaky 3G connections in rural areas.

Audio-First Briefings: "The Morning Pod"—AI-generated daily market summaries delivered in a polished, charismatic Ghanaian or Ivorian accent for the executive on the move.

⚖️ 4. Compliance & "Jollof" Diplomacy
Skills for navigating the complex web of international trade and regional cooperation.

EUDR Regulatory Copilot: A "Sanctions & Standards" engine that automatically flags bean lots at risk of missing the new EU Deforestation Regulation deadlines.

The Abidjan-Accra Accord Integration: Real-time updates on the partnership between Ghana and Côte d'Ivoire, ensuring users are never blindsided by joint-nation supply shifts.

Direct-to-Chocolatier Matchmaking: A B2B skill set that connects small-scale "Exotic" bean farmers directly with boutique European and American craft chocolate makers.

🛠 Technical Stack Highlights
Framework: Flutter/Dart for that fluid, "Magazine-style" UI seen in the design.

ML Engine: Python-based forecasting models (Prophet/XGBoost) tuned for agricultural seasonality.

Data Source: Integration with COCOBOD APIs, Euronext, and proprietary field agent nodes.
