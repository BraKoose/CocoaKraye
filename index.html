The Foundational Challenge

Prohibitive cost and technical complexity keep smallholders and cooperatives from accessing the digital intelligence required for modern compliance—especially EUDR—and precision agriculture.

High cost to digitize
Soil testing, GPS mapping, and monitoring are fragmented and expensive at scale.
Hard to operationalize
Raw field data rarely becomes standardized, validated, and decision-ready.
Regulatory pressure
Due diligence, traceability, and sustainability proof are now table stakes.

Why it matters

Without foundational intelligence, systems fail upstream.

Traceability platforms and compliance tools are only as good as the farm data beneath them. Cocoa Kraye focuses first on acquiring validated foundational datasets.
